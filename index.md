# Trap Copilot

Your personal trap shooting coach, calculator, and score tracker.

Trap Copilot helps trap shooters of all skill levels improve their game with interactive tools, lead calculations, score tracking, miss analysis, and coaching tips — all based on official ATA specifications.

## Features

- **Station Guides** — Lead calculations and aim points for every station and target angle
- **Shot Analysis** — Detailed sight pictures with rib-aware aim points
- **Score Tracker** — Track rounds, analyze trends, and estimate your ATA classification
- **Miss Analysis** — Log and analyze your miss patterns with coaching recommendations
- **Learn** — Practice drills, fundamentals, eye dominance assessment, and more

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**
A: No. All features work offline. Your data is stored on your device.

**Q: Does Trap Copilot collect any data?**
A: No. We do not collect, transmit, or share any data. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Go to Settings > Data Management > Clear All Scores. You can also delete and reinstall the app.

**Q: Does the app support Skeet or Sporting Clays?**
A: Trap Copilot focuses exclusively on American Trap. Skeet support will be available in a separate app.

**Q: How accurate are the lead calculations?**
A: Lead values are approximations based on ATA target specifications and standard physics. Actual lead varies with conditions, reaction time, and shooting style. See our [Terms of Service](/terms).
