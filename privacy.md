# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 21, 2026
**App:** Trap Copilot
**Developer:** RF Animation LLC

## Summary

Trap Copilot does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

- **User Profile** — Name, shooting hand, eye dominance, experience level, handicap yardage, ATA member number
- **Shooting Scores** — Round results, shot details, miss analysis, weather conditions, event names
- **Equipment** — Gun specifications, shell preferences, choke information
- **Miss Analysis** — Miss direction logs and patterns
- **Preferences** — App settings (auto-save, experience level)

## Data Not Collected

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising or ad identifiers
- No third-party SDKs, libraries, or services
- No network requests of any kind
- No location data
- No camera or photo library access
- No account or login required

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time:

1. Open Trap Copilot
2. Go to **Settings > Data Management > Clear All Scores**
3. To remove all data completely, delete the app from your device

## Children's Privacy

Trap Copilot does not collect data from any users, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
